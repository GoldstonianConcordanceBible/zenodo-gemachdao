# Chapter 8 — The Leadership Code
**Outline (3–5 bullets)**
- Leadership wasn’t charisma—it was consistency and correction.
- The “code”: what was enforced, tolerated, modeled.
- Leadership as repetition: same standard on good days and bad days.
- Culture protection: boundaries, truth, fairness.
- Call to action: live the code.

Every strong place has a code.

At GBC, it was lived.

The code:
- The standard is the standard.
- Respect is non-negotiable.
- Coachability beats talent.
- Work is the currency.
- Accountability is love.
- Fairness over favorites.

Leadership is holding the line under pressure.
