# Chapter 10 — The Cage Is a Classroom
**Outline (3–5 bullets)**
- The cage exposes timing, focus, ego, and work ethic.
- The cage is the safest place to fail—and the best place to learn.
- Beyond mechanics: discipline, patience, coachability.
- How the standard turns practice into formation.
- Call to action: treat practice like class.

The cage teaches:

When the cage is protected as a classroom, it produces self-control—not just swings.
